My Journey

I was born and raised in China, where I developed a deep curiosity about the natural world. My academic journey led me to explore biology, particularly gene regulation and functional genomics. Over time, I have blended experimental and computational approaches to investigate biological questions, leading to research published in top journals.

Academic Interests

My research focuses on gene regulation, 3D genome organization, and single-cell analysis. I am particularly interested in integrating deep learning with genomics to uncover complex biological mechanisms.

Beyond Science

Outside the lab, I am a passionate calligrapher, particularly fascinated by the works of Wang Xizhi and Mi Fu. I also enjoy playing the alto saxophone and exploring cross-disciplinary intersections between art and science.

Who I Am

People often describe me as curious, analytical, and creative. I have a deep appreciation for both structured logic and artistic expression, which reflects in both my research and hobbies. I also value humor and enjoy engaging conversations.
